How do I edit an user account?



To edit an user account from the app:

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to the user section and click on login.



  3. Fill in all the form fields. All are mandatory.



  4. Click on the blue login button. Once the form is completed without errors, you can edit your account.
  5. Go to the user section and click on the pencil (view first image).
  6. Fill in the form field you wish to edit and then click on edit profile.
