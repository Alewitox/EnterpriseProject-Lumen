How do I do a booking?



To do a booking from the app:

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to the user section and log in. You cannot reserve a product if you have not logged in before.
  3. Fill in all the form fields. All are mandatory.



  4. Go to the home section and click on the product you wish to book.



  5. Then we will see the description of the product and its price. To check availability press the check availability button.



  6. Finally, we choose the date of the calendar on which we want to make the reservation and click on the book button.



    7. The product has been reserved. You can see the reservations in the user section.
