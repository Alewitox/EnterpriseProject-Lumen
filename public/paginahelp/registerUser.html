How do I create an user account?



To create an user account from the app:

  1. Download the app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Once the app is installed, tap hotel image to open it.
  3. Then, go to the user section and click on Register



  4. Fill in all the form fields. All are mandatory.



  5. Finally, click on register blue button. Once the form is completed without errors, you will be redirected to login
